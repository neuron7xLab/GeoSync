**Law T3 — Coupling calibration to a target maximal Lyapunov exponent.** New `core/kuramoto/lyapunov_calibration.py` ships `calibrate_coupling_to_lambda(target, *, omega, A, theta_0, …)` which finds the scalar `K* > 0` minimising `|λ_1(K · A; ω) − target|²` on a bounded interval. Composes Law T1 RHS with Law T2 estimator under SciPy's bounded Brent. Returns `CalibrationReport(status, K_optimal, lambda_achieved, residual, n_evaluations)` with `CalibrationStatus.CONVERGED` or `INFEASIBLE`. Constitutional invariants `INV-CAL1` (residual ≤ tolerance for feasible target), `INV-CAL2` (INFEASIBLE on impossible target — never silent best-effort), `INV-CAL3` (K* > 0 always; every contract violation raises ValueError). Negative control proves the optimiser does not silently clamp to K_min.
