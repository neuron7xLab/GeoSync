Added `core.physics.calibration` — a one-function-call calibration harness for P0 physics invariants. Generates synthetic data with known ground truth (Davies-Harte fBm for INV-DRO1; analytical Ott–Antonsen R_∞ for INV-OA2), runs the production estimator, and emits a self-describing `CalibrationReport` with pass/fail verdict against documented spec tolerances. Default grid: 9 cases, 9/9 pass. CLI entry: `python -m core.physics.calibration`.
